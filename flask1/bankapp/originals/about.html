Our Goal

Bank of America is comprised of many businesses which in turn, are comprised of many applications, services, and processes. Those applications, services, and many other sub-branches underneath the business may have dependencies between them. The purpose of this project is to visualize these dependencies in a clear and consise platform as well as map the impact these connections have on one another. Each tab is a way to visualize the current status of a business.
